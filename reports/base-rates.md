# CAA base rates — computed from raw data

Arrival rows only. Years ingested: [2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025].
Generated by `wizarb base-rates`; tables in `reports/*.csv`.

**Direction caveat:** CAA records delay at the _UK reporting airport_, so
arrival rows are inbound legs (e.g. Bucharest→Luton). The outbound leg's
EU261-relevant delay (arrival at the foreign destination) is only visible
here as its UK _departure_ delay — a proxy that overstates arrival delay by
the ~5–15 min typically recovered in the air. Cell screening should be run
both ways.
